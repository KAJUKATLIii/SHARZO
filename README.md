--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT IS SHARZO?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SHARZO is a powerful screen mirroring and control application that lets you
mirror, record, and control your Android device directly from your Windows PC.
Designed specifically for content creators, streamers, and mobile gamers who
want to showcase their Android gameplay or apps on a bigger screen.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KEY FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[+] REAL-TIME SCREEN MIRRORING
    - Mirror your Android screen to your PC with minimal latency
    - Smooth 60 FPS streaming for gaming and app demos
    - Supports high resolutions up to your device's maximum

[+] FULL DEVICE CONTROL
    - Control your Android device using your mouse and keyboard
    - Perfect for mobile gaming with PC controls
    - Type using your PC keyboard - faster than on-screen typing

[+] SCREEN RECORDING
    - Record your Android screen directly to your PC
    - Capture gameplay, tutorials, or app demonstrations
    - Save recordings in high quality for editing or streaming

[+] SCREENSHOT CAPTURE
    - Take instant screenshots of your device
    - Save directly to your PC for easy sharing

[+] WIRELESS & USB CONNECTION
    - Connect via USB for zero-lag gaming
    - Connect wirelessly over Wi-Fi for flexibility
    - Seamlessly switch between connection modes

[+] AUDIO FORWARDING
    - Stream your device's audio to your PC
    - Perfect for game sound effects and music
    - Syncs perfectly with video for recording/streaming

[+] ADVANCED DISPLAY OPTIONS
    - Crop and resize the mirrored screen
    - Rotate display for landscape/portrait games
    - Fullscreen mode for immersive gaming
    - Always-on-top option for multitasking

[+] MULTIPLE DEVICE SUPPORT
    - Connect and switch between multiple Android devices
    - Great for developers and content creators with multiple devices

REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  - Windows PC (Windows 10/11 recommended)
  - Android device with USB debugging enabled
  - USB cable or Wi-Fi network for connection
  - ADB drivers installed (included)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
QUICK START
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Enable USB Debugging on your Android device:
   Settings > Developer Options > USB Debugging

2. Connect your device via USB (or Wi-Fi for wireless)

3. Run sharzo.exe

4. Your device screen will appear on your PC!
